About Me

I'm a robotics engineer and PhD student with a strong focus on mobile robotics, computer vision, and SLAM. I have hands-on experience in ROS, AI, and embedded systems, and I enjoy turning complex robotic problems into practical solutions.

I'm passionate about research and development, and I regularly share my knowledge through projects, blogs, and tutorials. My goal is to become an expert in robotics and contribute to real-world applications that make a difference.
